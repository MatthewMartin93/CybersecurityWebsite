Phishing Awareness

Phishing attacks trick users into sharing private data or clicking dangerous links.

Be cautious! Check sender addresses, grammar, and links.
  • Never click suspicious links or attachments.
  • Verify the sender’s identity.
  • Report suspicious emails to IT/security.
